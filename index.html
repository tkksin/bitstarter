There is a very high mortality rate in children due to hunger. As per the UNICEF survey the estimated number of childhood deaths in 2010 was 7.6 million. At least half of the 7.6 million child deaths are due to poor nutrition. Almost one out of every 15 children will die before they reach the age of five in developing countries. The main cause of child hunger is poverty and at the same time hunger also casues poverty; By causing poor health, low levels of energy, and even mental impairment.

Every child deserves a decent childhood with some basic education and food. This project is targeted to all the children in this world who are not fortunate enough like us and to achevie this every other fortunate kid can help. Parents can enroll their kids into this program where in kids can showcase their ART and put them on AUCTION. All the money from the AUCTION will go to the KIDS in REAL NEED. A KID can Save KID
